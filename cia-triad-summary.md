CIA Triad Summary

Confidentiality

Definition

Confidentiality means information is only accessible to authorized people.

Real-World Example

Only the HR department can view employee salary records.

Control

Access controls, encryption, and Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).

---

Integrity

Definition

Integrity means information remains accurate and is not changed without authorization.

Real-World Example

A bank account balance cannot be altered by unauthorized users.

Control

Hashing, digital signatures, and change management.

---

Availability

Definition

Availability means systems and data are accessible when needed.

Real-World Example

An online banking website is available for customers 24/7.

Control

Backups, disaster recovery plans, redundant servers, and system monitoring.

---

Summary

Confidentiality = Keep data secret.

Integrity = Keep data accurate.

Availability = Keep systems running.
